The F.G. Degree College for Men in Peshawar Cantt was established in 1964 and initially operated as an intermediate college. It was later upgraded to degree level, and its administration was transferred to the Federal Ministry of Education. Currently, the college is managed by the Directorate of Federal Government Educational Institutions (Cantt/ Garrison), which falls under the Ministry of Defence.
KEY EVENTS IN COLLEGE'S HISTORY:
1964: Established as C.B. Intermediate College.
1975: Administrative control transferred to the Federal Ministry of Education after the "Nationalization" of Cantt. Boards' Educational Institutions.
1977: Control and management shifted to the General Headquarters (GHQ) under the umbrella of the Ministry of Defence.
1978: Upgraded to degree level.
1992: All Federal Govt. Educational Institutions (Cantt/Garrison) were handed over to the Ministry of Defence.
2007: F.G. Degree College for Boys Multan Cantt was established, also under the Directorate of FGEI (C/G).
2011: Government of Pakistan converted federal government colleges in Islamabad into model colleges.
